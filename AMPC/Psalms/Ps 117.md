# Psalms 117

***














###### 1 






O praise the Lord, all you nations! Praise Him, all you people! 













###### 2 






For His mercy _and_ loving-kindness are great toward us, and the truth _and_ faithfulness of the Lord endure forever. Praise the Lord!

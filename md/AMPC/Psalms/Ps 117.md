# Psalms 117

***


###### 1 


O praise the Lord, all you nations! Praise Him, all you people! 


###### 2 


For His mercy and loving-kindness are great toward us, and the truth and faithfulness of the Lord endure forever. Praise the Lord! (Hallelujah!)

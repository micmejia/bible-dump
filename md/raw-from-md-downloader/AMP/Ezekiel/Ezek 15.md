# Ezekiel 15

***
















###### 1 







And the word of the LORD came to me, saying, 















###### 2 







"Son of man, how is the wood of the grapevine (Israel) better than any wood of a branch which is among the trees of the forest? 















###### 3 







Can wood be taken from it to make any object? Or can men take a peg from it on which to hang any vessel? 















###### 4 







If it has been thrown into the fire for fuel, and the fire has consumed both of its ends and the middle section has been charred, is it suitable _or_ useful for anything? 















###### 5 







Notice this, even when it was complete, it was not useful _and_ was not made into anything. How much less, after the fire has burned [part of] it and [the remainder of] it is charred, can it still be made into anything? 















###### 6 







Therefore, thus says the Lord God, 'Like the wood of the grapevine among the trees of the forest, which I have given to the fire for fuel, so have I given up the people of Jerusalem; 















###### 7 







and I set My face against them. _Though_ they have come out of the fire, yet the fire will consume them. Then you will know [without any doubt] that I am the LORD, when I set My face against them.

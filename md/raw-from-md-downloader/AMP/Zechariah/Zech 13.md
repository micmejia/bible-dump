# Zechariah 13

***
















###### 1 







"In that day a fountain shall be opened for the house of David and for the people of Jerusalem for [cleansing from] sin and impurity. 















###### 2 







"In that day," declares the LORD of hosts, "I will cut off the names of the idols from the land, and they will no longer be remembered. I will also remove the [false] prophets and the unclean spirit from the land. 















###### 3 







And if anyone still [appears as a prophet and falsely] prophesies, then his father and his mother who gave birth to him will say to him, 'You shall not live, for you have spoken lies in the name of the LORD'; and his father and his mother who gave birth to him shall pierce him through when he prophesies. 















###### 4 







And in that day the [false] prophets will each be ashamed of his vision when he prophesies, and they will not wear a hairy robe [of true prophets] in order to deceive, 















###### 5 







but he will [deny his identity and] say, 'I am no prophet. I work the ground, because a man sold me as a slave in my youth.' 















###### 6 







And one will say to him, 'What are these [^a]wounds between your arms?' Then he will answer, 'Those wounds I received in the house of my friends.' 















###### 7 







"Awake, O sword, against My Shepherd, And against the Man, My Associate," Declares the LORD of hosts. "Strike the Shepherd so that the sheep [of the flock] may be scattered; And I will turn My hand _and_ stretch it out against the little ones [of the flock]. 















###### 8 







"It will come about in all the land," Declares the LORD, "Two parts in it will be cut off and perish, But the third will be left alive. 















###### 9 







"And I will bring the third part through the fire, Refine them as silver is refined, And test them as gold is tested.

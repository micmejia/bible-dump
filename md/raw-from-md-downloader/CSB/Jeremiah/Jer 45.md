# Jeremiah 45

***










###### 1 




This is the word that the prophet Jeremiah spoke to Baruch son of Neriah when he wrote these words on a scroll at Jeremiah's dictation[^a] in the fourth year of Jehoiakim son of Josiah, king of Judah: 









###### 2 




"This is what the Lord, the God of Israel, says to you, Baruch: 









###### 3 




'You have said, "Woe is me, because the Lord has added misery to my pain! I am worn out with[^b] groaning and have found no rest."' 









###### 4 




"This is what you are to say to him: 'This is what the Lord says: "What I have built I am about to demolish, and what I have planted I am about to uproot--the whole land! 









###### 5 




But as for you, do you pursue great things for yourself? Stop pursuing!

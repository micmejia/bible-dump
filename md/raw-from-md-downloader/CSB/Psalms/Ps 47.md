# Psalms 47

***










###### 1 




Clap your hands, all you peoples; shout to God with a jubilant cry. 









###### 2 




For the Lord, the Most High, is awe-inspiring, a great King over the whole earth. 









###### 3 




He subdues peoples under us and nations under our feet. 









###### 4 




He chooses for us our inheritance-- the pride of Jacob, whom he loves.<i class="selah">Selah_ 









###### 5 




God ascends among shouts of joy, the Lord, with the sound of a ram's horn. 









###### 6 




Sing praise to God, sing praise; sing praise to our King, sing praise! 









###### 7 




Sing a song of wisdom,[^a] for God is King of the whole earth. 









###### 8 




God reigns over the nations; God is seated on his holy throne. 









###### 9 




The nobles of the peoples have assembled with the people of the God of Abraham.

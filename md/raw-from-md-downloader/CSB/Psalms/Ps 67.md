# Psalms 67

***










###### 1 




May God be gracious to us and bless us; may he make his face shine upon us<i class="selah">Selah_ 









###### 2 




so that your way may be known on earth, your salvation among all nations. 









###### 3 




Let the peoples praise you, God; let all the peoples praise you. 









###### 4 




Let the nations rejoice and shout for joy, for you judge the peoples with fairness and lead the nations on earth.<i class="selah">Selah_ 









###### 5 




Let the peoples praise you, God, let all the peoples praise you. 









###### 6 




The earth has produced its harvest; God, our God, blesses us.

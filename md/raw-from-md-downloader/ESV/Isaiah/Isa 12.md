# Isaiah 12

***






###### 1 


You[^a] will say in that day: "I will give thanks to you, O LORD, for though you were angry with me, your anger turned away, that you might comfort me. 





###### 2 


"Behold, God is my salvation; I will trust, and will not be afraid; for the LORD God[^b] is my strength and my song, and he has become my salvation." 





###### 3 


With joy you[^c] will draw water from the wells of salvation. 





###### 4 


And you will say in that day: "Give thanks to the LORD, call upon his name, make known his deeds among the peoples, proclaim that his name is exalted. 





###### 5 


"Sing praises to the LORD, for he has done gloriously; let this be made known[^d] in all the earth. 





###### 6

# Job 25

***








###### 1 



Then Bildad the Shuhite responded, 







###### 2 



"Dominion and awe [^a]belong to Him Who makes peace in His heights. 







###### 3 



Is there any number to His troops? And upon whom does His light not rise? 







###### 4 



How then can mankind be righteous with God? Or how can _anyone_ who is born of woman be pure? 







###### 5 



If even the moon has no brightness And the stars are not pure in His sight, 







###### 6

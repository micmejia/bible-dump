# Psalms 121

***








###### 1 



I will raise my eyes to the mountains; From where will my help come? 







###### 2 



My help _comes_ from the LORD, Who made heaven and earth. 







###### 3 



He will not allow your foot to slip; He who watches over you will not slumber. 







###### 4 



Behold, He who watches over Israel Will neither slumber nor sleep. 







###### 5 



The LORD is your [^a]protector; The LORD is your shade on your right hand. 







###### 6 



The sun will not [^b]beat down on you by day, Nor the moon by night. 







###### 7 



The LORD will [^c]protect you from all evil; He will keep your soul.

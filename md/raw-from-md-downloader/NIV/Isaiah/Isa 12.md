# Isaiah 12

***




















###### 1 









In that day you will say: "I will praise you, LORD. Although you were angry with me, your anger has turned away and you have comforted me. 



















###### 2 









Surely God is my salvation; I will trust and not be afraid. The LORD, the LORD himself, is my strength and my defense[^a]; he has become my salvation." 



















###### 3 









With joy you will draw water from the wells of salvation. 



















###### 4 









In that day you will say: "Give praise to the LORD, proclaim his name; make known among the nations what he has done, and proclaim that his name is exalted. 



















###### 5 









Sing to the LORD, for he has done glorious things; let this be known to all the world. 



















###### 6 









Shout aloud and sing for joy, people of Zion, for great is the Holy One of Israel among you." NIV Reverse Interlinear Bible: English to Hebrew and English to Greek.

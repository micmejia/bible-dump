# Malachi 4

***




















###### 1 









[^a]"Surely the day is coming; it will burn like a furnace. All the arrogant and every evildoer will be stubble, and the day that is coming will set them on fire," says the LORD Almighty. "Not a root or a branch will be left to them. 



















###### 2 









But for you who revere my name, the sun of righteousness will rise with healing in its rays. And you will go out and frolic like well-fed calves. 



















###### 3 









Then you will trample on the wicked; they will be ashes under the soles of your feet on the day when I act," says the LORD Almighty. 



















###### 4 









"Remember the law of my servant Moses, the decrees and laws I gave him at Horeb for all Israel. 



















###### 5 









"See, I will send the prophet Elijah to you before that great and dreadful day of the LORD comes. 



















###### 6 









He will turn the hearts of the parents to their children, and the hearts of the children to their parents; or else I will come and strike the land with total destruction."

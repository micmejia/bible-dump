# Psalms 122

***




















###### 1 









I rejoiced with those who said to me, "Let us go to the house of the LORD." 



















###### 2 









Our feet are standing in your gates, Jerusalem. 



















###### 3 









Jerusalem is built like a city that is closely compacted together. 



















###### 4 









That is where the tribes go up-- the tribes of the LORD-- to praise the name of the LORD according to the statute given to Israel. 



















###### 5 









There stand the thrones for judgment, the thrones of the house of David. 



















###### 6 









Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: "May those who love you be secure. 



















###### 7 









May there be peace within your walls and security within your citadels." 



















###### 8 









For the sake of my family and friends, I will say, "Peace be within you." 



















###### 9 









For the sake of the house of the LORD our God, I will seek your prosperity. NIV Reverse Interlinear Bible: English to Hebrew and English to Greek.

# Psalms 127

***




















###### 1 









Unless the LORD builds the house, the builders labor in vain. Unless the LORD watches over the city, the guards stand watch in vain. 



















###### 2 









In vain you rise early and stay up late, toiling for food to eat-- for he grants sleep to[^a] those he loves. 



















###### 3 









Children are a heritage from the LORD, offspring a reward from him. 



















###### 4 









Like arrows in the hands of a warrior are children born in one's youth. 



















###### 5 









Blessed is the man whose quiver is full of them. They will not be put to shame when they contend with their opponents in court. NIV Reverse Interlinear Bible: English to Hebrew and English to Greek.

# Psalms 134

***




















###### 1 









Praise the LORD, all you servants of the LORD who minister by night in the house of the LORD. 



















###### 2 









Lift up your hands in the sanctuary and praise the LORD. 



















###### 3 









May the LORD bless you from Zion, he who is the Maker of heaven and earth. NIV Reverse Interlinear Bible: English to Hebrew and English to Greek.
